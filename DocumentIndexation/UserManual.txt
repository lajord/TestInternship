---
User Manual for Online Banking Services
---

1. Account Creation
To create an account, visit our website and click on "Open an Account." Follow the instructions and provide the necessary documents, such as a valid ID and proof of address.

2. Managing Bank Cards
You can manage your bank cards through your online account. Activate, block, or replace your cards with just a few clicks. For added security, set up transaction alerts.

3. Transfers and Payments
To make a transfer, go to the "Transfers" section of your account. You can also schedule recurring payments. For international transfers, you will need the beneficiary's bank details.

4. Support and Security
If you encounter any issues, contact our customer service. To enhance your account security, enable two-factor authentication. Regularly update your contact information to ensure you receive important notifications.
